What is a VPN?

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) like LASLES VPN creates a secure connection between your device and the internet. it encrypts your data, hides your IP address, and allows you to browse online anonymously. VPNs are essential tools for safeguarding privacy, bypassing geo-restrictions, and avoiding censorship.
